Peppers
(Capsicum spp.)
Annual or perennial flowering plants in the nightshade family, known for their colorful fruits used as vegetables or spices. They thrive in warm, sunny environments with well-drained soil.

Peppers come in various types, including bell peppers, chili peppers, and ornamental varieties like the Thai dragon fruit pepper. The plants are prone to pests like aphids and diseases such as bacterial spot.
