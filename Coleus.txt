Coleus
(Solenostemon scutellarioides)
Fast-growing, tropical flowering plants in the mint family, known for their vibrant foliage with a range of colors and patterns. They prefer partial shade and moist soil.

Coleus has two main types: ornamental species grown for their attractive leaves and cultivars used for culinary purposes. The plants are susceptible to pests like spider mites and diseases such as leaf spot.
